Mood Diary
Mood Diary is diary that visualize your flow of mood and thoughts from recored input. This diary have input of time, event, comments and drop down menu to select your mood for each event. So at the end of the day, you can record what you did on that day, your thoughts and your feelings. On graph page, you can see daily data and monthly data and a graph. The graph will depict emotions like pleasure, passion, calmness, anxiety, tiredness and depression in color and over time. Graphs will display a day or week or month.
